Donating to charities

We are fortunate enough to have everything we need. A contribution to your favorite charity in our honor is a wonderful way to commemorate our special day. We have listed a few of our favorite charities below. All of our chosen charities have been recognized for using their donations effectively.

Please click here to let us know that you're giving in our name! We don't have it set up to automatically tell us. Thank you!

Union of Concerned Scientists

The Union of Concerned Scientists focuses on applying scientific research and methods to develop and implement innovative, practical solutions to the earth's most pressing problems, from slowing climate change to developing sustainable agriculture and transportation.

The Electronic Frontier Foundation

The EFF is a nonprofit working to defend equal access to the internet. Currently, their main project is reinstating net neutrality, which kept access to all parts of the internet equally available.


Registry

If you would like to give a gift, we are registered with Honeyfund, a website that allows giving experiences in addition to physical things. (The whole John Lewis thing didn't work out - we didn't love their selection.) If you decide to give a donation to a charity in our name, please don't also get us a physical gift!
